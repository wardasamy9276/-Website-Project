FAQ

What is included in the tours?

All tours include transportation, accommodation, and guided services unless specified otherwise.

How do I book?

You can book online through our website or contact our support for assistance.

Are the tours family-friendly?

Yes! Our tours are suitable for families with children of all ages.

Do I need travel insurance?

We recommend travel insurance for all trips, but it is not mandatory.

What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel up to 7 days before the tour for a full refund.

Are meals included?

Some tours include meals; please check the tour details for specifics.

Can I customize my tour?

Yes, we offer customizable tours based on your preferences.

What languages are the tours available in?

Our tours are available in English, Arabic, and other languages on request.
